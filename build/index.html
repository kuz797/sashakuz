About Me

“Hello, world!” "👋，🌍！" "你好，世界!" "Привет, мир!" I'm Sasha Kuzmenko, a senior a nerd a swimmer an artist
at Glenda Dowson High School and an aspiring mechanical engineer.

Here's my TL;DR
Back in 2022, I decided to indulge my love for coding, and apply to MIT's Women's Technology Program. Specifically, their Electrical Engineering and Computer Science track. I soon realized it was on hiatus, and instead, I applied to the Mechanical Engineering track. Five months later, I was on a plane to Cambridge. During the month, I tumbled head first into the world of engineering and design - where I fell in love.

Fast-forward to today, and in my free time I've bought my own soldering kit, built IKEA furniture, created this website (how meta), and started 3D printing stuff out of my friend's garage (sorry Mikayla's dad).

When I'm not “creating,” I might be reading a book, listening to bossanova, or reinforcing walls in Rainbow Six Siege Ra in bo w   Si x   Si eg e with my friends.
